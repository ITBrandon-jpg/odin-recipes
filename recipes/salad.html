Cobb Salad Recipe

This is a super simple to make Cobb Salad recipe!

Following the instructions below on how to make a simple but amazing tasting Cobb Salad.

Ingredients List

  • 6 Slices of bacon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 3 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • 3/4 cups blue cheese, crumbled
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 avacado - peeled, pitted and diced
  • 1 (8 ounce ) bottle Ranch-style salad dressing

Steps

  1. Step 1: Place eggs in a saucepan and cover it completely with cold water; bring it to a boil, then cover and remove from the heat. Let eggs sit for 10 to 12 minutes, then cool, peel and chop.

  2. Step 2: While the eggs are cooking, place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown, 7 to 10 minutes. Drain, crumble, and set aside.

  3. Step 3: Divide shredded lettuce among individual plates. Top with rows of bacon, eggs, chicken, tomatoes, blue cheese, green onions, and avacado.

  4. Step 4: Drizzle the dressing.
